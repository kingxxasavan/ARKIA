Here is the completely revised, highly optimized version of ARIA tailored strictly for **GLM-4.6 via Ollama**. I have stripped out the heavy, unnecessary logic for other providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter) to make it highly efficient. The memory system has been rebuilt into a single, reliable stream that automatically learns from your conversations and saves them.
